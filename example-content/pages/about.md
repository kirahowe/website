This is a personal weblog: posts, links, and quotes, organized by
date and tag.

It's built with [Clojure](https://clojure.org) running under
[babashka](https://babashka.org), rendering a folder of markdown files.
The [content](https://github.com) and the [code](https://github.com) live
in separate repositories.
